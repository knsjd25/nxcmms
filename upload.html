Free Secure & Temporary Image Hosting

Mini-Tools provides a specialized free image hosting service built entirely for your privacy. Unlike standard image hosting websites that permanently store and analyze your metadata, our "Auto-Destruct" mechanism ensures that your shared files are physically erased from our global Cloudflare R2 storage nodes after your chosen duration (1 to 30 days).

How our Secure Upload works?

We utilize edge computing architecture to handle your files. When you click upload, your image is securely transmitted via TLS encryption to the nearest edge node. If you have a VIP ID, your files are routed to a dedicated permanent storage zone; otherwise, the automated lifecycle policy takes over, ensuring the file is purged once its time expires. This makes it the perfect tool for sharing sensitive debugging screenshots, temporary social media assets, or forum images.
