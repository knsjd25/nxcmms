Last Updated: March 2026

At Mini-Tools.uk, accessible from https://mini-tools.uk, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Mini-Tools.uk and how we use it.

1. The "Client-Side Processing" Guarantee

The core philosophy of Mini-Tools.uk is absolute data privacy. Unlike traditional web services, all calculations, image processing, and text manipulations are executed entirely within your web browser (Client-Side). This means:

  • When you calculate your UK PAYE Tax, your salary inputs are never transmitted over the internet.
  • When you use our Image Color Picker, your images are rendered locally via HTML5 Canvas and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • We do not have backend databases to store, log, or analyze the contents you process using our utilities.

2. Use of LocalStorage

To improve your user experience and enable offline capabilities, we may utilize your browser's LocalStorage. For instance, our Currency Converter caches the latest fetched exchange rates locally so the tool can function without an active internet connection. This data is strictly stored on your own device and is not accessible by our servers.

3. Third-Party Services and Cookies (Google AdSense)

While our utility tools process data locally, our website relies on standard third-party services for analytics and monetization, which may use cookies:

Google AdSense and DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our site's visitors based upon their visit to Mini-Tools.uk and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL – https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

We use Google AdSense Advertising on our website. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites.

4. Log Files

Mini-Tools.uk follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and it is a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at hello@mini-tools.uk.
