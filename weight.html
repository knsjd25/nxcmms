Why the UK still uses stones

In everyday life, people in the UK often describe body weight using stones and pounds. In healthcare, fitness apps and many international contexts, the same value is usually recorded in kilograms. This converter helps switch between the two systems quickly without rounding surprises.

This page is designed for simple conversion only. It does not judge whether a weight is good, bad, healthy or unhealthy.

  • Total pounds = stones × 14 + pounds
  • Kilograms = total pounds × 0.45359237
