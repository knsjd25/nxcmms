Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are there any hidden fees or commissions added to the rate?

No. Our calculator provides the "mid-market rate"—the midpoint between the buy and sell prices of two currencies. This is the real, exact exchange rate. Please note that banks, credit cards, and money transfer services usually add a markup (spread) to this rate when you actually exchange money.

How accurate is the offline mode?

The offline mode relies on the exact rates from the last time you accessed the tool with an internet connection. Since major fiat currencies typically fluctuate by only fractions of a percent daily, the cached rates are highly reliable for general travel budgeting and retail shopping estimations.
