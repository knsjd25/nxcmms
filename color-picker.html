Advanced Client-Side Image Color Picker & Cropper

Welcome to the ultimate privacy-focused image utility suite. Whether you are a frontend developer extracting exact UI colors, or a content creator needing to quickly crop a screenshot, our dual-purpose tool processes everything locally. No server uploads, no data retention.

How to use the Workspace

  • Auto-Fit Canvas: When you upload a large screenshot, our tool automatically zooms the image to fit your screen. You can use the floating zoom controls in the bottom right corner to magnify specific areas.
  • Color Picker Mode: Hover your cursor over the canvas and click on any pixel. The specific HEX and RGB codes will populate in the top-right console.
  • Screenshot Mode: Drag your mouse over any area. Confirm your selection to instantly download the cropped area as a high-quality PNG file.

纯本地运行的图片颜色提取与截图裁剪工具

这是为设计师和开发者量身定制的隐私保护型图像工具。无论你是需要精准提取 UI 元素的 HEX 代码，还是需要快速框选并裁剪局部图片，我们的双效合一工具都能在本地瞬间完成。绝无服务器上传，100% 保护隐私。

核心功能特性

  • 智能缩放自适应： 即使上传超长的网页截图，系统也会自动缩放图片以适应你的屏幕。你可以通过右下角的控制台自由放大（Zoom In）去精准提取像素。
  • 取色模式： 在下方画布中点击任意像素。顶部的控制台会实时显示并记录精确的 HEX 与 RGB 代码。
  • 局部截图模式： 拖拽画出高亮选区。点击确认后，选中的区域将立刻作为高画质 PNG 格式自动下载。
