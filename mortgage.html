What is LTV (Loan-to-Value) and Why Does it Matter?

LTV (Loan-to-Value) is one of the most important metrics used by UK banks and building societies (like Barclays, Halifax, and Nationwide). It represents the ratio of the loan amount compared to the total value of the property.

For example, if you buy a £300,000 house and put down a deposit of £60,000, you need a mortgage of £240,000. Your LTV is calculated as: (£240,000 / £300,000) * 100 = 80%. Generally, a lower LTV grants you access to cheaper, more competitive interest rates because the lender takes on less risk. Our calculator features a traffic-light LTV indicator to instantly show you if your deposit ratio is healthy.

Repayment vs. Interest-Only Mortgages

  • Repayment (Capital & Interest): This is the standard mortgage type. Every month, you pay back a portion of the original loan amount (the capital) plus the interest accumulated. By the end of your term (e.g., 25 years), your balance will be zero, and you will own the property outright.
  • Interest-Only: Your monthly payments only cover the interest charged on the loan. While these monthly payments are significantly lower, your original loan amount remains completely untouched. You must have a credible repayment vehicle (like an investment portfolio or plans to downsize) to pay off the capital lump sum at the end of the term.

核心概念：什么是 LTV (贷款成数)？

LTV (Loan-to-Value) 是英国各大银行（如 Barclays, HSBC, Halifax）评估贷款风险的最重要指标。它代表了您的贷款金额占房产总价值的百分比。

例如，如果您购买一套价值 £300,000 的房产，首付了 £60,000，那么您需要贷款 £240,000。您的 LTV 就是：(£240,000 / £300,000) * 100 = 80%。通常情况下，LTV 越低（即您的首付比例越高），银行承担的风险就越小，您能申请到的利率就越低。我们的工具内置了 LTV 健康度指示器，帮您瞬间判断当前的资金结构是否安全。

还款方式对比：Repayment 与 Interest-Only

  • 本息同还 (Repayment)： 这是最标准的房贷类型。您每个月的还款额中包含了偿还的部分本金和产生的利息。在贷款期限（例如 25 年）结束时，您的贷款余额将归零，您将 100% 拥有该房产。
  • 仅还利息 (Interest-Only)： 您每个月只需要支付贷款产生的利息。虽然每月的还款压力会大幅降低，但您的原始贷款本金丝毫未减。在贷款期结束时，您必须通过其他方式（如出售房产、投资变现）一次性偿还全部本金。
