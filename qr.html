What are the different types of QR Codes?

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) is a two-dimensional matrix barcode that can store significantly more data than a standard UPC barcode. Our free online QR generator is optimized to create highly functional "Smart Codes" for various scenarios:

  • URL / Web Links: The most common type. Instantly redirects the user's smartphone to your website, social media profile, or an online PDF menu.
  • Wi-Fi Access: Stop forcing guests to type complex passwords. A Wi-Fi QR code automatically authenticates and connects the scanning device to your local network.
  • vCard (Digital Business Card): Encodes your name, phone number, and email. When scanned, the smartphone automatically prompts the user to save you as a new contact in their address book.

Understanding QR Error Correction (ECC)

Have you ever wondered why some QR codes still work perfectly even when they are dirty, scratched, or have a logo placed in the middle? This is due to the Reed-Solomon Error Correction Algorithm. On our tool, you can manually select from 4 recovery levels:

  • Level L (Low - 7%): Creates the simplest, least dense pattern. Best for codes displayed on clear digital screens where damage is impossible.
  • Level M (Medium - 15%): The standard default for most general purposes.
  • Level Q (Quartile - 25%): Recommended for printed materials in controlled environments.
  • Level H (High - 30%): Creates a very dense pattern but allows nearly a third of the code to be obscured. Absolutely essential for codes printed on physical goods, outdoor posters, or if you plan to digitally edit a logo into the center.

深入了解二维码 (QR Code) 及其应用场景

二维码是一种二维矩阵条码，其数据存储能力远超传统的条形码。我们的免费在线二维码生成器针对多种实际应用场景提供了专门的“智能模板”：

  • URL 网址链接： 最常见的类型。扫描后立即将用户的手机重定向至您的网站、微信公众号文章或在线菜单。
  • Wi-Fi 一键连接： 别再让客人费力输入复杂的路由器密码了。Wi-Fi 二维码能让手机扫描后直接进行身份验证并自动连入您的网络。
  • vCard (电子名片)： 可以在方寸之间编码您的姓名、电话和邮箱。扫描后，手机通讯录会自动弹出“新建联系人”的提示。

为什么有的二维码损坏了还能扫？解析“容错率”

这归功于二维码内置的 Reed-Solomon 纠错算法 (Error Correction)。在本工具中，您可以手动指定 4 个容错等级：

  • 等级 L (7% 恢复)： 生成的图案最稀疏、最简洁。适合仅在电脑或手机屏幕上显示的无损环境。
  • 等级 M (15% 恢复)： 日常使用的标准默认值。
  • 等级 Q (25% 恢复)： 推荐用于传单、易拉宝等常规印刷品。
  • 等级 H (30% 恢复)： 允许高达 30% 的面积被遮挡。如果您打算把二维码印在容易磨损的户外海报、商品外包装上，或者打算在二维码正中间 P 上您公司的 Logo，必须选择此等级。
