What are the different types of QR Codes?

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) can store various types of information. Our free online QR generator is optimized for:

  • URL QR Codes: Link directly to your website, social media, or online store.
  • Wi-Fi Access: Share your home or office network details effortlessly.
  • Text & Information: Perfect for sharing simple notes or business descriptions.

Understanding QR Error Correction (ECC)

Ever wondered why some QR codes still work when they are slightly damaged? This is due to Error Correction Levels. On our tool, you can choose from 4 levels:

  • Level L (Low): Allows 7% of the code to be damaged. Best for clean, digital use.
  • Level H (High): Allows up to 30% recovery. Essential for codes printed on physical goods that might get scratched or worn.

如何选择二维码的容错率？

在生成二维码时，容错率 (Error Correction Level) 是一个关键参数。它决定了二维码在被部分遮挡或损毁的情况下，依然能够被扫描出来的能力。

  • 低容错 (L)：适合在屏幕上显示的二维码，数据密度最高。
  • 高容错 (H)：适合印刷在包装、户外海报上，即使二维码有 30% 的损伤也能正常读取。
