How this QR generator helps

A QR code is a quick way to move from paper or screen into an action on a phone. This page supports the most common use cases: website links, Wi‑Fi access, email actions, and a simple contact card. Everything is generated locally in your browser, so the entered data does not need to be sent away for processing.

Is it private?

Yes. QR code generation happens locally in your browser and the input is not sent to our servers.

Will the QR code expire?

The image itself does not expire. It keeps working as long as the encoded destination or content remains valid.

What does error correction do?

Higher levels make the QR code more resilient to damage or obstruction, but they also increase visual density.

Can I use it for Wi‑Fi and contact info?

Yes. The built-in templates make it easier to format Wi‑Fi, email, and vCard-style contact QR codes correctly.
