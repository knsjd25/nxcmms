How does our Secure Password Generator work?

Your security is our priority. Unlike other online tools, Mini-Tools.uk uses the Web Crypto API, a cryptographically secure random number generator built into your modern browser. This ensures that your passwords are 100% random and, more importantly, never leave your device.

What makes a password strong in 2026?

A strong password should be a combination of complexity and length. Security experts recommend a minimum of 12 to 16 characters. By mixing uppercase letters, numbers, and special symbols, you increase the entropy, making it virtually impossible for brute-force attacks to succeed.
