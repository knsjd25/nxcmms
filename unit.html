Metric vs. Imperial: Why do we need conversion?

While most of the world uses the Metric system (meters, kilograms, liters), the Imperial system (inches, pounds, gallons) is still widely used in the USA and for certain applications in the UK. Our online unit converter helps you bridge this gap instantly.

Temperature Conversion: Celsius to Fahrenheit

Converting temperature is unique because it's not just a simple multiplier. The formula for Celsius to Fahrenheit is $(^\circ C \times 9/5) + 32$. Our tool handles these complex calculations for you, including Kelvin for scientific needs.

为什么需要单位换算？

全球大部分地区使用公制单位（米、千克、升），但在美国和英国的部分领域，英制单位（英寸、磅、加仑）依然非常普遍。Mini-Tools.uk 提供的在线单位换算器能帮您秒速完成转换。

常见换算公式

  • 长度：1 米 (m) ≈ 3.28 英尺 (ft)
  • 重量：1 千克 (kg) ≈ 2.2 磅 (lb)
  • 温度：华氏度 (°F) = (摄氏度 × 9/5) + 32
