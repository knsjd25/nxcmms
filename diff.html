What is an Online Diff Checker?

A Diff Checker is a tool used to find the difference between two pieces of text or code. It highlights additions in green and deletions in red. Our tool is optimized for Word Diff, meaning it tracks changes down to the individual words for maximum precision.

什么是文本对比工具？

文本对比工具（也称为 Diff Checker）用于找出两段文字或代码之间的差异。它会以绿色高亮新增内容，以红色高亮删除的内容。本工具采用单词级对比 (Word Diff)，确保每一个微小的改动都能被精准捕捉。
