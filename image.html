Large images are the primary cause of slow website performance. Our online image resizer utilizes advanced canvas rendering to reduce file size without sacrificing clarity. Optimized for 2026 standards, we support WebP—the format Google loves for superior SEO performance.

Why Mini-Tools is Different?

Unlike other tools that send your data to remote servers, we process everything on the client-side. Your photos stay on your device, making this the most secure way to handle private documents or personal photos.

为什么选择 Mini-Tools 进行图片调优？

过大的图片是影响网页加载速度的核心因素。我们的工具采用 Web Crypto API 级别的隐私标准，所有图片均在浏览器本地处理，绝不上传云端。
