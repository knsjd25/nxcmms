Why this image tool is useful

Unoptimized images are one of the fastest ways to slow down a website or increase upload size. This page lets you resize images, switch aspect ratios, lower file size, and export to a more efficient format without sending files to a remote server. It is a simple local workflow for content publishing, design prep, and general image cleanup.

Is it private?

Yes. The image is processed locally in your browser and is not uploaded to our servers.

Can I crop to social media ratios?

Yes. You can switch between original, 1:1, 4:3, 16:9, and 9:16 aspect ratios.

Should I use JPG or WebP?

WebP is often smaller at similar quality, while JPG remains a widely compatible option.

Will it keep the image sharp?

It aims to preserve clarity while reducing size, but the final result depends on the original image and your quality setting.
