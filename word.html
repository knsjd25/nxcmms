Why is Word Count important for SEO?

In the world of Search Engine Optimization (SEO), content length is a known factor that influences search rankings. While quality is king, long-form content (usually over 1,500 words) tends to rank better for complex keywords. Our free online word counter helps you ensure your blog posts, meta descriptions, and articles meet the target length.

Reading and Speaking Time Estimation

We use an average reading speed of 200 words per minute (wpm) and a speaking speed of 130 wpm. This is essential for speechwriters and video script creators to gauge their presentation length accurately.

Data Privacy

At Mini-Tools.uk, we prioritize your security. All text analysis is performed locally in your browser. We never store or transmit your text, making it 100% secure for sensitive documents.
